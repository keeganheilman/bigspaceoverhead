torch-stunt

A "read" stunt performed by two interior defensive linemen (iDL).
The two iDL read the Center. When the Center turns toward the iDL, that iDL loops to the opposite A Gap.
Meanwhile, the iDL from which the Center turned away impedes the Center from tracking the looping iDL.
