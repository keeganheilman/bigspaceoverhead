#+title: wedge-stunt
-----

A "read" stunt occurring between edge player and interior defensive lineman. \\
Edge player reads the offensive Tackle (OT). If his set is deep, the Edge player folds to the A gap behind the iDL, who simulateously is working to broaden the B Gap.
